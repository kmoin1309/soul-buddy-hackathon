YOGA ASANAS

Trikonasana

It helps in reducing excess body weight, strengthens legs and arms, and improves digestion.

Virabadrasana

It strengthens legs, improves balance, enhances focus, and instills determination.

Vrikshasana

It improves the strength of your back and legs and enhances balance.

Bhujangasana

It improves blood circulation and strengthens the lower back and arm muscles.

Sukhasana

It calms the mind, enhances peace, and improves body posture.

Chakrasana

It cures back and kidney pain and stimulates the thyroid and pituitary glands.

Balasana

This pose is mentally, physically, and emotionally relaxing.

Shavasana

It relaxes the entire body, improves memory, and increases concentration.
